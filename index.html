ROOM TYPES

Single Occupancy Rooms

A single occupancy room is a room that is intended for one person to sleep in, and is often found in hotels or rented accommodations. It typically has a single bed, chair, and sometimes a small desk.

Family Rooms

A Family Room can often mean a Standard Room with space for a sofa bed or camp bed. It does not always allow space for a sofa bed and a travel cot at the same time so make sure to check with the hotel directly if travelling with a baby and a toddler or child.

Duplex Rooms

Duplex room, the duplex suite comprises two rooms situated different floors which are connected by an internal staircase.
